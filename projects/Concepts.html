★ How to Disagree

质疑与争论的境界：(from best to worst)

  • refuting the central point(反驳主要论点）
  • refutation(反驳原文，原话)
  • counter-argument(驳斥)
  • contradiction(反对)
  • responding to tone(批评对方语气)
  • ad hominem(质疑身份地位)
  • name-calling(辱骂)

★ Induction VS Deduction

  • A good induction goes from observations to general statements, the strength of the argument and the evidence will raise the Probability of the truth.
  • A good deduction goes from basic statements to additional statements until you hit a conclusion. The conclusion is definitely true if the premises are true
